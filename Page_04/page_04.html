About Us

Our Humble Beginnings

In the early 2010s, Sairam, inspired by a void he noticed between football and food experiences, embarked on a journey with his close group of football friends from university to bridge this gap. Fueled by a shared passion for the beautiful game and a desire to enhance the overall football-watching experience, they founded our company. From those humble beginnings, we've grown into a dynamic team dedicated to bringing innovation and joy to the intersection of sports and cuisine.

Our mission

Looking ahead, our vision is to expand the reach and impact of our unique concept that intertwines the worlds of football and food. We envision establishing meaningful partnerships, opening more outlets, and creating larger spaces where enthusiasts can come together to celebrate their love for the game while indulging in delightful culinary experiences. Our mission is to continually grow, fostering an environment where the connection between football and food thrives, creating lasting memories for fans and friends alike.
